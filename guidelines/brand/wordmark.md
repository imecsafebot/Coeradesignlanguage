# Wordmark & Brand Mark

## Wordmark (Logo)

The coera wordmark is built on **circular foundations** — precise, repeating forms creating balance, flow, and continuity. It feels both **engineered and human**.

### Clearspace

Clearspace around the wordmark must equal the **height of the "c" in coera** on all sides. This ensures the mark stays clear, legible, and free from visual clutter.

### DO NOTs — IMPORTANT

Never do any of the following to the wordmark:

- **No outline** — don't apply stroke/outline effects
- **No drop shadows** — keep it flat and clean
- **No rotation** — always keep it level
- **No distortion** — don't stretch, squash, or warp
- **No swapping elements** — don't rearrange letterforms
- **No multi-color** — don't apply gradients or multiple colors
- **No recoloring** — use as-is (cream on dark, near-black on light)
- **No spacing changes** — don't tweak letter spacing

## Brand Mark (Icon)

Two interlocking circular forms representing **continuity, unity, and infinite exchange** between human and machine.

### Clearspace

Minimum **30% space** around the brand mark icon on all sides.

### Usage

- Use the brand mark where the full wordmark doesn't fit (app icons, favicons, small formats)
- Never pair the brand mark with a different wordmark

## Color Rules

```
┌─ "What color for the wordmark?"
├─ On black/dark background?
│  └─ Cream (#F8F1ED)
└─ On cream/light background?
   └─ Near-black (#292424) or Black (#000000)
```

## Partner Logo Pairing

When displaying the coera wordmark alongside partner logos:
- Give both brands room to breathe
- Scale proportionally
- Align with care
- Create a balanced, collaborative appearance
