# Brand Voice & Messaging

## Voice

coera speaks with **clarity, authority, and intent**.

| Quality | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Bold** | Confident, assertive, category-defining |
| **Visionary** | Forward-looking, inspiring, future-oriented |
| **Credible** | Grounded, technically accurate, trustworthy |

- Technically fluent — speaks the language of engineers and integrators
- Commercially credible — resonates with executives and investors
- Forward-thinking — positions coera as a category creator
- Safety is an **enabler**, never a constraint

## Brand Name

Always lowercase: `coera`. Never "Coera" or "COERA".
Exception: start of a sentence where grammar requires it.
